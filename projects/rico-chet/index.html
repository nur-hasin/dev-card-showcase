📖 Vector Physics Manual

The Engine:

This game uses a custom-built 2D physics engine utilizing Vector mathematics and Dot Products to calculate perfect geometric reflections off angled walls.

How to Play:

  1. Aiming: Click and drag anywhere on the screen (or directly on the ball) to pull back your club.
  2. Power: The further you drag away from the ball, the longer the aiming line becomes, representing the velocity vector.
  3. Hazards: Dark green areas are "Rough" (high friction). Blue areas are "Water" (out of bounds, resets your ball).
  4. The Hole: The ball will only fall into the black hole if it is moving slowly enough. If you blast it at top speed, it will lip out!
