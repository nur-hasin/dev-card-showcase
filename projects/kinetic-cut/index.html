📖 Physics Lab Manual

The Goal:

Deliver the glowing blue energy orb into the green target zone at the bottom of the screen.

How to Play:

  1. The Swipe: Click and hold your left mouse button, then drag across the screen to create a slice line.
  2. Cut the Rope: Drag your slice line directly *through* the white ropes to sever them.
  3. Timing: If the orb is swinging, cut the rope at the exact right moment so momentum carries it into the target.
  4. Score: Earning a win grants you 100 points and unlocks the next level on the left grid!
